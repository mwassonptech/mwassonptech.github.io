Mousetrap Car

This was one of the first projects that I did as an Excelsior Student. This was a difficult project at first, but it became easier once we all knew the design. What we had to do was, make a car that runs on a mousetrap and non-electronic parts. The requirements for this project was that it had to go at least 15 feet. My groups car ended up going 18 feet. Like any other project, my group faced some difficulties. In order to overcome these difficulties, my group and I stayed after school to build it and worked on designing at home.

Public Speaking Project

This was the first public speaking project that I did as an Excelsior Student. The goal of this was to get us more used to public speaking. We had to make a 2-4 minute speech based on a chosen topic. My topic that I chose was gun control.

White House Film Festival

During the summer, one of the projects that we worked on was a short film. This project was not only meant for us to get a "feel" for working with computers but, it was also meant to allow us to get to know our fellow peers. All the groups consisted of Year 1 and Year 2 students. I look back on this project as the very first project that I did as an Excelsior Student. My topic for the project was Poverty and Education.
