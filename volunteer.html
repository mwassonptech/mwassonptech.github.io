Eel Counting

Every Thursday, our English and Science teacher pick a couple of students to go to the Hudson River and count eels. It it a citizen science juvenile eel monitoring program that observed the number of juvenile eels in tributaries of the Hudson River. Along with DEC staff, trained volunteers, and teachers we checked specialized nets weekly for eels. The project provides crucial baseline data on young eel populations in the Hudson River, and gets students and community members into their local streams. This is a link to the Report.
