About Me

My name is Matthew Wasson. I am in my second year as a student at Excelsior Academy, a New York State P-TECH high school. I am earning my way towards a high school diploma and an Associate Degree in Applied Science. To demonstrate my commitment to academics, I have been a National Junior Honor Society member since 8th I've done various volunteering events which included volunteering at food shelters, pet shelters and expos at school. I'm currently in a few after school clubs. One of the clubs that I am in is a gaming design club. Another club that I will be joining this year is robotics. One day I hope to be designing games/apps. I plan on accomplishing this goal by working really hard and staying focused on my work. In my free time I play sports and spend time with friends. I'm currently taking two college classes which are College Success and Career and College Math 101. During the summer of 2017, I was given the opportunity to take a college coding course for General Assembly and it took me a couple of months to complete but, in the end it was all worth it. Taking this course has given me a better understanding about coding. During the duration of this course, I learned many things, one of the things that I learned was how to manage my time between school and other activities.
